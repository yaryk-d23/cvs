Live Failover Testing Requirements {{ctrl.currYear}}

The CVS Health Disaster Recovery Program requires that Active/Active and Active/Passive applications conduct Failover Exercises to test the process and hardware that would be used in a true recovery event. The scope, methodology and length may vary depending upon application capability and business impact.

Objectives:
v  Leverage the process and hardware that would be used in a true recovery event
v  Provide evidence of resilience by quickly moving mission critical application workload(s) between data centers minimizing impacts to the business
v  Successfully perform testing on recovered systems as documented in the Application Test Plans
v  Coordinate test dates with existing maintenance windows to minimize impacts to the business
v  Where possible, align testing with dependent failover capable applications to test integration functionality
v  All testing must be completed by September 30, {{ctrl.currYear}}
v  EDR Team will distribute a Quarterly Executive Summary highlighting the results of the applications tested in the previous quarter and provide notification of the upcoming Failover exercises
