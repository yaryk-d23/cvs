Live Failover Testing Requirements{{ctrl.currYear}}

The CVS Health Disaster Recovery Program requires that Active/Active and Active/Passive applications conduct Failover Exercises to test the process and hardware that would be used in a true recovery event. The scope, methodology and length may vary depending upon application capability and business impact.

  • Leverage the process and hardware that would be used in a true recovery event
  • Provide evidence of resilience by quickly moving mission critical application workload(s) between data centers minimizing impacts to the business
  • Successfully perform testing on recovered systems as documented in the Application Test Plans
  • Coordinate test dates with existing maintenance windows to minimize impacts to the business
  • Where possible, align testing with dependent failover capable applications to test integration functionality
  • All testing must be completed by September 30, 2021
  • It is important to adhere to completion dates as the results of these exercises must be included in the Q4 readout to the Board of Directors/Audit Committee.
  • EDR Team will distribute a Quarterly Executive Summary highlighting the results of the applications tested in the previous quarter and provide notification of the upcoming Failover exercises
